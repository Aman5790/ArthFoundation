Diet advisory

  • Balanced diet rich in antioxidants minerals & vitamins mainly from fresh Fruits and vegetables
  • Low calorie diet in case you are over weight
  • Reduce oil and sugar intake

Exercise advisory

  • Quadriceps drills
  • Joint mobilization
  • Walk
  • Yoga/Gym
  • Weight loss exercises

Precautions

  • Weight reduction
  • Avoid sitting cross legged
  • Avoid squatting
  • Avoid frequent stairs climbing

Medicine

  • Pain Relievers (if needed)
  • Local pain relieving ointments / gels
  • Visit a physiotherapy center for atleast 15 days, then followup exercises at home

  • Visco Supplemntation (for Osteoarthritis) Lubricants Injections inside the knee joints
