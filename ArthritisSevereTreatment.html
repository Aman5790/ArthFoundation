Diet advisory

  • Balanced diet rich in antioxidants minerals & vitamins, Fruits & vegitables
  • Low calorie diet incase you are over weight

Exercise advisory

  • Quadriceps drills
  • Joint mobilization
  • Walk
  • Yoga/Gym
  • Weight loss exercises

Precautions

  • Weight reduction
  • Avoid sitting cross legged
  • Avoid squatting

Medicine

  • Pain Relievers (if needed)
  • Local pain relieving ointments
  • Visit a physiotherapy center for atleast 15 days
  • Joint Lubricants
  • Glucosamine
