Tips

Apart from Arthritis and Osteoporosis backache and neck pain are very common in the modern work schedules.

How do we manage Back Pain?

  1. Maintaining proper body weight
  2. Avoid lifting heavy objects.
  3. Use the legs rather than the back lifting
  4. Maintain proper posture while sitting and standing, do not slouch.
  5. Use a chair with arm rests. Rise from the bed by first rolling to one side and then using the arms to push to an upright position.
  6. Taking medication for pain regularly as advised.
  7. Avoid prolonged sitting in one go.

How to manage Neck pain (Postural or Cervical Spondylosis)?

  1. Neck exercises (in consultation with doctor and physio).
  2. May use cervical collar for acute pain and vertigo.
  3. Painkillers and muscle relaxants like tab: crocin, volini or moov gel
  4. Avoid driving car but if driving, wear a cervical collar and avoid any jerky movements as it may trigger pain.
  5. Avoid bending neck for long hours, after every two hour take a 5-10 minutes break and carry out neck exercise prescribed by the physiotherapist.
  6. Neck traction at physiocentre helps
  7. Short wave diathermy or (TENS) may provide pain relief.
  8. Surgery needed only in severe and neurological involvement cases
