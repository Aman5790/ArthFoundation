Osteoporosis

What is Osteoporosis?

Osteoporosis is not just an "old woman's" disease. It is now a silent epidemic, especially targeting women and senior citizens.


Osteoporosis is a disease in which the density and quality of bone is reduced, leading to weakness of the skeleton and increased risk of fracture, particularly of the spine, wrist, hip, pelvis and upper arm. Osteoporosis and associated fractures are an important cause of mortality and morbidity.
