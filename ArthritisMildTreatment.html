Treatment

Treatment for mild Arthritis needs

  1. Diet advisory
  2. Exercise advisory
  3. Precautions
  4. Medicines

Diet advisory

  • Balanced diet rich in antioxidants minerals & vitamins, fruits & vegetables
  • Low calorie diet incase you are over weight

Exercise advisory

  • Quadriceps drills
  • Joint mobilization
  • Walk
  • Yoga/Gym
  • Weight loss exercises

Precautions

  • Weight reduction
  • Avoid sitting cross legged
  • Avoid squatting

Medicine

  • Pain Relievers (if needed)
  • Local pain relieving ointments
  • Physiotherapy at home
