Prevention

Easy steps to prevent Arthritis

  1. Diet
  2. Exercise
  3. Advisory

Diet

  • Balanced diet rich in antioxidants minerals & vitamins mainly from fresh Fruits and vegetables
  • Low calorie diet in case you are over weight
  • Reduce oil and sugar intake

Exercise

  • Quadriceps drills
  • Joint mobilization
  • Walk
  • Yoga/Gym
  • Weight loss exercises

Advisory

  • Weight reduction
  • Avoid sitting cross legged
  • Avoid squatting
