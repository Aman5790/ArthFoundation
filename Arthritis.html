Arthritis

What is Arthritis?

Arthritis literally means joint inflammation. But the term is often used to refer to any of the more than 100 diseases that affect the joints. Joint is articulation of two or more bones to allow movement.

What is Osteoarthritis?

It is the degenerative type of Arthritis, most common and majority of elderly citizens are sufferers. Here the joint surfaces get damaged and the adjoining bone gets thicker.
